Joey DeGori's Website Page: Featuring Taco

Taco is an eight year old boston terrier with a white and black coat of hair. He often has the zoomies and frequently tries to fight his brother, Pickles.

Taco has MANY Pastimes:

  • Playing with toys
  • Bothering Pickles
  • Eating and sleeping
  • Running as fast as possible

Taco's Favorite Toys Include:

  1. Fuzzy colorful ball
  2. Bright pink dinosaur
  3. Green duck

Taco is a playful dog who loves his outside time. He often loves to sunbathe and be in fresh air. With his love of the outside comes a great hatred for any sort of wind or rain. As long as it is a windless sunny day, Taco will love to explore the great outdoors.
